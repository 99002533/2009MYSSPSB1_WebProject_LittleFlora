Programs

Our Mindful curriculum EUNOIA is designed to help your child learn & enhance life skills with specially crafted multi-level kindergarten school programs. Flora Pre-School curriculum is full of fun activities. Musical experiences, storytelling, DIY kits and a healthy mix of indoor & outdoor play ensures your child gets the best pre-school experience possible.

Playgroup(1.8 to 3 years)

Hands-on discovery and exploration with exclusive EuroKids kits Developing language skills Encouraging learning of new age skills Engaging children physically, emotionally and socially Shaping scientific thought in children

Nursery(2.5 to 4 years)

Imparts essential life skills Enhances learning and interaction through fun with exclusive EuroKids kits Cultivates creativity & Develop curiosity Improves imagination through Art and Music

FloraJunior(3.5 to 5 years)

Special activities for developing language skills Age appropriate learning with exclusive EuroKids learningtools Encourages children to express their ideas, thoughts and feelings Allows children to grow at their own pace

FloraSenior(4.5 to 6 years)

Building & enhancing vocabulary Developing attention span Innovative methods to enhance reading & writing with exclusive EuroKids Kits Preparing children for primary school

International Yoga Day

Yoga is a light which once lit will never dim, the better your practice the the brighter your flames.Little Flora PreSchool wishes you Happy International Yoga Day. Children began the Yoga Day celebrations with a warm up session, followed by different yoga poses like standing, sitting and lying down poses. Educators taught some common yoga postures to children like the archer's pose, mountain pose, jumping jacks, bowing pose, etc.
